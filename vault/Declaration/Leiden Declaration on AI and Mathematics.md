# Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics

A June 2026 declaration warning that artificial intelligence threatens the core values of mathematics — proof, verification, attribution, peer review and human oversight — and calling on mathematicians, institutions, policymakers and tech companies to disclose AI use, protect authorship, preserve peer review and keep research independent of commercial pressure.

## Facts

- **Website:** https://leidendeclaration.ai
- **DOI:** 10.5281/zenodo.20302944
- **Published:** 2026-06-02
- **Origin:** Grew out of a September 2025 workshop at the Lorentz Center, Leiden University (60 participants from 10 countries).
- **Working group lead:** Jim Portegies (Eindhoven University of Technology / TU/e)
- **Endorsement:** International Mathematical Union (IMU)
- **Signatories:** 2,500+
