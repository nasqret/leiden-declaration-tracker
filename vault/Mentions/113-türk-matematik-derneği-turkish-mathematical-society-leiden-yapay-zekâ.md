# Leiden Yapay Zekâ ve Matematik Bildirgesi

> **Outlet:** [[Türk Matematik Derneği (Turkish Mathematical Society)]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Author:** Türk Matematik Derneği (Turkish Mathematical Society) · **Date:** 2026-06 · **Language:** Turkish · **Country:** [[Turkey (country)\|Turkey]]

## Summary

Official page of the Turkish Mathematical Society (Türk Matematik Derneği, an IMU member society) announcing and presenting a Turkish translation of the 2026 "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics." The page explains that the declaration emerged from a broadly-attended conference at the Lorentz Center (Leiden), was shared with the public in June, and calls for protecting the core values of mathematics (integrity of mathematical proof, transparency, merit, academic autonomy) in the face of AI, with recommendations for mathematicians, institutions, funders, policymakers, and the commercial AI sector. A full Turkish PDF is provided (hosted under /wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Leiden_YZ_Bildirgesi_0.pdf) and the International Mathematical Union is referenced.

## Key quote
