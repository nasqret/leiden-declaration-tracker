## Summary

Science-section coverage in Le Monde of the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics: mathematicians launch an appeal to regain control over AI. The piece notes the declaration has been signed by more than 2,300 researchers, identifies five threats (notably to researchers' autonomy and to the evaluation/peer-review process), is endorsed by the International Mathematical Union, and does not call for banning AI but urges clear community norms for its use.
