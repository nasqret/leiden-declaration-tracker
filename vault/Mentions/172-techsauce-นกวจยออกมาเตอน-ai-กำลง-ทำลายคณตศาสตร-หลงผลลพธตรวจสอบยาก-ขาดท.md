## Summary

Techsauce, a Thai technology-news outlet, published a Thai-language article explicitly covering the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics. It explains that the declaration addresses AI's impact on mathematics, emphasizing it is not an anti-AI/ban document but rather questions what responsible AI use in mathematics should look like, citing concerns that AI-generated results are hard to verify and lack clear provenance/attribution. The piece notes the declaration was signed by 16 mathematicians plus over 130 additional signatories and references Gizmodo as a source. It does not name individual signatories (Tao, Scholze, LeCun), Jim Portegies, or the IMU, and does not use the "don't believe the hype" line. Confirmed via WebFetch that the page explicitly references THIS 2026 declaration (not the 2015 Leiden Manifesto).
