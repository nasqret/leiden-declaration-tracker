# AI rozwiązało słynny problem geometrii. Matematycy ogłaszają deklarację

> **Outlet:** [[WP Tech (Wirtualna Polska)]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** Kamil Łęcki · **Date:** 2026-06-19 · **Language:** Polish · **Country:** [[Poland (country)\|Poland]]

**Link:** https://informacje.wp.pl/technologie/ai-rozwiazalo-slynny-problem-geometrii-matematycy-oglaszaja-deklaracje-7298276259379232a

## Summary

Polish-language technology article from WP Tech (Wirtualna Polska), one of Poland's largest digital media portals. It reports on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, framing it against the backdrop of OpenAI's announcement that AI solved the unit distance problem in combinatorial geometry. Rather than celebrating, mathematicians voiced concern over AI's threat to scientific integrity. The article lays out the declaration's five core points: mandatory disclosure of AI use in research; peer review of all machine-generated proofs; equal treatment of academic and commercial actors; enhanced verification of AI-generated proofs; and independent funding so mathematicians retain control of research direction. It notes the concern that mathematicians' work is being exploited for AI development without their consent, and that the declaration seeks to balance innovation with transparency and verifiability.

## Key quote

> Matematycy, którzy nigdy nie zamierzali przyczyniać się do rozwoju AI, widzą, jak ich prace są wykorzystywane do tego celu bez ich zgody.
