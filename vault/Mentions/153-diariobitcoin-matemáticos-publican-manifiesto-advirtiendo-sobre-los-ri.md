# Matemáticos publican manifiesto advirtiendo sobre los riesgos de la IA en su disciplina

## Summary

Spanish-language article reporting on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, an 11-page document signed by 130+ mathematicians warning against irresponsible AI use in the field. It notes the declaration grew out of a September workshop at Leiden University's Lorentz Center, was developed by a 16-member working group including Jim Portegies, is backed by the International Mathematical Union, and raises concerns about AI-generated claims lacking peer review, poor attribution, and threats to rigorous proof and verification.

## Key quote

> Los sistemas actuales pueden generar argumentos plausibles, pero poco fiables o incorrectos.
