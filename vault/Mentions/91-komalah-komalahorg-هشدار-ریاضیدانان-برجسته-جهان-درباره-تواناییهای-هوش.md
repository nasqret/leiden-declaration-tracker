## Summary

A Persian-language news item reporting that more than 150 prominent mathematicians worldwide published and signed the "Leiden Declaration" (بیانیه لیدن), urging governments, universities, and the public to adopt a more realistic assessment of AI systems' capabilities and limitations. It relays the declaration's warnings that AI can generate convincing but false or fabricated answers and that overreliance threatens scientific peer review and evaluation, also noting concerns about AI weaponization and surveillance.

## Key quote

> مدل‌های هوش مصنوعی می‌توانند پاسخ‌هایی ظاهراً متقاعدکننده تولید کنند که در واقع نادرست یا ساختگی هستند ("AI models can generate seemingly convincing answers that are in fact incorrect or fabricated")
