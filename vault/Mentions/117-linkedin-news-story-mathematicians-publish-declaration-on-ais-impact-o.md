# Mathematicians publish declaration on AI's impact on math

> **Outlet:** [[LinkedIn News (story)]] · **Type:** 💬 Social media · **Author:** LinkedIn News editorial team (curated story; no individual byline) · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English

**Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/news/story/mathematicians-publish-declaration-on-ais-impact-on-math-7324876/

## Summary

A LinkedIn News curated "story" aggregating coverage of the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, published June 2, 2026. It describes the declaration as seeking to address AI's growing influence on mathematics, raising concerns about accuracy, attribution and ethics, emphasizing transparency, peer review and collaboration, and warning governments not to "believe the hype" about AI's mathematical abilities.

## Key quote

> [The declaration] seeks to address the growing influence of artificial intelligence on mathematics, and raises concerns about accuracy and ethical implications... It aims to ensure that the values of the math community are upheld as AI continues to evolve.

## Significance

LinkedIn News is a major professional-network aggregator with very large reach (LinkedIn News stories surface to millions of users), giving the declaration significant business/professional visibility.
