# Wiskundigen slaan alarm: wat gebeurt er met wiskunde in het tijdperk van AI?

> **Outlet:** [[HCC]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Date:** 2026-06-10 · **Language:** Dutch · **Country:** [[Netherlands (country)\|Netherlands]]

**Link:** https://hcc.nl/kennis/kennis/reviews/wiskundigen-slaan-alarm-wat-gebeurt-er-met-wiskunde-in-het-tijdperk-van-ai

## Summary

A Dutch-language article from HCC (the Dutch Hobby Computer Club) reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. It explains that mathematicians warn AI offers great opportunities but simultaneously puts fundamental mathematical values under pressure, citing risks such as AI producing superficially correct proofs containing subtle errors, missing or incomplete citations, researcher dependency on commercial black-box systems, and research agendas shifting toward commercial rather than scientific relevance.

## Key quote

> In de zogeheten Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics waarschuwen zij dat AI grote kansen biedt, maar tegelijkertijd fundamentele waarden van de wiskunde onder druk zet.

## Significance

HCC (Hobby Computer Club) is one of the largest and oldest Dutch consumer computing/technology associations, giving the declaration broad reach to a general Dutch tech-enthusiast audience. The article appears to be a staff/editorial review piece (no individually notable author bylined); reach is moderate-to-major within the Netherlands consumer-tech sphere rather than an academic or international outlet.
