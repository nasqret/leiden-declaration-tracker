# Mathematiker: KI-Hype nicht unkritisch übernehmen

> **Outlet:** [[Epoch Times Deutsch]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** AFP (wire agency) · **Date:** 2026-06 · **Language:** German · **Country:** [[Germany (country)\|Germany]]

**Link:** https://www.epochtimes.de/wissen/mathematiker-ki-hype-nicht-unkritisch-uebernehmen-a5508586.html

## Summary

German-language coverage (an AFP wire story republished by Epoch Times Deutsch) of the Leiden Declaration. It reports that more than 150 mathematicians warned against uncritically adopting the "hype" around the mathematical capabilities of AI, noting that AI developers have a strong commercial incentive to exaggerate their products and that AI solutions to individual math problems are misleadingly presented as benchmarks for general capabilities. It cites mathematician Michael Harris (Columbia University) on the industry's economic pressure and stresses that mathematics' future should rest on human judgment, fair and transparent procedures, and the shared values of the global mathematical community.

## Key quote

> In einer am Dienstag, 2. Juni, nach einer Konferenz in der niederländischen Universitätsstadt Leiden veröffentlichten Erklärung hieß es, KI-Entwickler hätten "einen starken kommerziellen Anreiz", die Fähigkeiten ihrer Produkte zu übertreiben.
