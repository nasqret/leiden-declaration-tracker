# Matematikçilerden yapay zeka uyarısı: Göründüğü kadar başarılı değil

## Summary

Turkish business daily Dünya Gazetesi reports on the "Leiden Yapay Zekâ ve Matematik Bildirisi" (Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics), warning that AI's success in mathematics is overstated because AI can produce convincing but incorrect proofs, and that mathematical results require expert human review before acceptance. It also relays the declaration's concerns about unauthorized use of academic work in AI training, hallucinated content entering the scientific literature, and broader harms (surveillance, misinformation, environmental impact).

## Key quote

> "İkna edici ama yanlış" sonuçlar üretebiliyor (AI can produce "convincing but incorrect" results)

Dünya Gazetesi is a long-established mainstream Turkish economy/business newspaper with a sizable national audience, so this represents notable Turkish-language coverage of the declaration.
