# Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics

> **Outlet:** [[leidendeclaration.ai]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Author:** Working group convened by Jim Portegies (Eindhoven University of Technology / TU/e), 14 members · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[Netherlands (country)\|Netherlands]]

**Link:** https://leidendeclaration.ai/

## Summary

This is the official website of the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. It presents the declaration, which calls on mathematicians to responsibly adopt AI while preserving core disciplinary values—proof, attribution, transparency, verification, and human autonomy—amid threats from unreliable automated arguments, improper citation, and industry influence over research priorities.

## Key quote

> Mathematical proofs are regarded as conferring the highest degree of certainty to their conclusions, as well as imparting understanding of why their conclusions are true.
