# Community Initiative by Mathematicians Already Endorsed by the International Mathematical Union

> **Outlet:** [[HUN-REN Alfréd Rényi Institute of Mathematics]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Date:** 2026-06-30 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[Hungary (country)\|Hungary]]

**Link:** https://renyi.hu/en/news/community-initiative-mathematicians-already-endorsed-international-mathematical-union

## Summary

News post by Hungary's HUN-REN Alfréd Rényi Institute of Mathematics highlighting the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, which it notes is endorsed by the International Mathematical Union. It stresses the declaration's emphasis on preserving core mathematical values — human understanding, transparency of proofs, and researchers taking responsibility for their results — as AI enters mathematical research.

## Key quote

> Mathematics is not merely about producing theorems, but about human understanding, the transparency of proofs, and researchers taking responsibility for their results.
