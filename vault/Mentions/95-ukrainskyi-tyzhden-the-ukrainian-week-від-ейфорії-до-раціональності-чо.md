# Від ейфорії до раціональності: чому у 2026 році відбувається переоцінка ролі штучного інтелекту

## Summary

An analysis piece in the Ukrainian weekly Tyzhden ("From Euphoria to Rationality: Why a Reassessment of AI's Role Is Happening in 2026") argues that the era of discussing AI in the language of euphoria or panic is ending, giving way to a duller, institutional conversation about certification, responsibility, authorship and verification. It anchors this thesis in two mid-June 2026 events: Dario Amodei's (Anthropic CEO) essay "Policy on the AI Exponential," and the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. The article explicitly states the Leiden Declaration was approved on 2 June by the International Mathematical Union and signed by thousands of scientists worldwide, that it grew out of the Lorentz Center "Mechanization and Mathematical Research" conference at Leiden University in September 2025, and that its authors warn modern AI tools can generate plausible but false mathematical proofs that are hard to distinguish from genuine ones. It frames both events as a "demystification" of AI.
