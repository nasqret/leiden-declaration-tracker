## Summary

Chinese-language news aggregation describing the 2026 Leiden Declaration (《莱顿宣言》) drafted by 16 mathematicians at Leiden University, reporting over 650 signatories by June 3. It covers the declaration's calls for mandatory disclosure of AI tool use in research, author accountability for paper contents, copyright protections for mathematical works used to train AI, and warnings against AI companies cherry-picking easily-solvable problems. A second half of the article covers an unrelated UN University environmental report on AI energy consumption.
