## Summary

The article reports on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics (Leidenská deklarace o umělé inteligenci a matematice), in which international mathematicians warn that AI threatens fundamental mathematical values such as rigorous proof, verification, and attribution. It centers on Czech signatory Vítězslav Kala of the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics at Charles University, who explains why he signed and notes that AI can produce convincing-looking but completely wrong mathematical texts.

## Key quote

> AI zdaleka není bezchybná. Dokonce umí vyprodukovat velmi přesvědčivě vypadající matematické texty, které jsou ale zcela chybné.
