# Mathematicians sign declaration to rein in AI use

> **Outlet:** [[Scientific American]] · **Type:** 🧪 Science & tech magazines · **Author:** Leila Sloman (edited by Clara Moskowitz) · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

**Link:** https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/mathematicians-sign-declaration-to-rein-in-ai-use/

## Summary

Reports on the release of the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, in which mathematicians, computer scientists, and historians establish guidelines to prevent AI from harming their field. Highlights key recommendations such as disclosing AI use in research, preserving peer review, and leveling the playing field between academia and for-profit companies.

## Key quote

> "Mathematicians who never intended to contribute to AI development are having their work used for this purpose without their consent" — Rodrigo Ochigame, anthropologist of AI at Leiden University
