# Les mathématiciens mettent en garde contre les menaces que fait peser l'IA sur leur profession

> **Outlet:** [[Developpez.net (forum)]] · **Type:** 🗣️ Forums & discussion · **Author:** Patrick Ruiz · **Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Language:** French · **Country:** [[France (country)\|France]]

**Link:** https://www.developpez.net/forums/d2184075/general-developpement/algorithme-mathematiques/intelligence-artificielle/mathematiciens-mettent-garde-contre-menaces-peser-l-ia-profession/

## Summary

This French-language forum thread (mirroring a Developpez.com news article) reports on the 2 June 2026 "Déclaration de Leyde sur l'intelligence artificielle et les mathématiques," noting it was developed over eight months by sixteen researchers following a September 2025 conference at Leiden University and was endorsed by the International Mathematical Union. It summarizes the declaration's five main concerns, including AI producing plausible but erroneous proofs, weak citation practices, disruption of academic hiring/funding, informal communication channels, and growing tech-company influence on mathematical research autonomy.

## Key quote

> Les mathématiciens devraient trouver assez frappant que les entreprises technologiques s'intéressent soudainement à leur travail (Kevin Buzzard, Imperial College London)
