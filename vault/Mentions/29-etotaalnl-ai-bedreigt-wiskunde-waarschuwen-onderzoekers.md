# AI bedreigt wiskunde, waarschuwen onderzoekers

> **Outlet:** [[etotaal.nl]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Date:** 2026-06-03 · **Language:** Dutch · **Country:** [[Netherlands (country)\|Netherlands]]

**Link:** https://etotaal.nl/nieuws/ai-bedreigt-wiskunde-waarschuwen-onderzoekers

## Summary

Dutch-language news article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, which grew out of a Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University and was endorsed by the International Mathematical Union. It describes the five key risks the declaration identifies (unreliable outputs, missing citations, dependence on closed commercial systems, inflated claims, loss of scientific independence) and notes that rather than banning AI, the mathematicians call for transparent usage policies, proper attribution, human accountability, and stronger regulation. Signatories named include Terence Tao and Robbert Dijkgraaf.

## Key quote

> Wiskunde is mensenwerk en moet dat altijd blijven (Mathematics is human work and must always remain so) - Ulrike Tillmann, IMU vice-president
