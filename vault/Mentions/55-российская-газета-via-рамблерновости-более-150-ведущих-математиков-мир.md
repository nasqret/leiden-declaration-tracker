# Более 150 ведущих математиков мира призвали не верить в "научные прорывы" ИИ

> **Outlet:** [[Российская газета (via Рамблер-новости)]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Date:** 2026-06-13 · **Language:** Russian · **Country:** [[Russia (country)\|Russia]]

**Link:** https://news.rambler.ru/tech/56598866-bolee-150-veduschih-matematikov-mira-prizvali-ne-verit-v-nauchnye-proryvy-ii/

## Summary

Reports that 150+ leading mathematicians from multiple countries issued the "Leiden Declaration" (Лейденская декларация), urging the scientific community and authorities not to accept AI developers' loud claims as genuine mathematical breakthroughs. It stresses that AI produces plausible-sounding but potentially erroneous reasoning, that an error at the foundation can corrupt entire chains of subsequent research, and that mathematical proof must still withstand rigorous expert peer review. The piece links the declaration to a wave of reports of LLMs allegedly solving long-standing problems (e.g., a young enthusiast claiming a ChatGPT-aided solution to an Erdős problem).

## Key quote

> математическое доказательство по-прежнему должно выдерживать строгую экспертную проверку (a mathematical proof must still withstand rigorous expert review)

Российская газета is the official Russian government daily newspaper, a major national outlet, giving the declaration wide reach in the Russian-language media; this version is further amplified via the Rambler news aggregator.
