# Matematikçilerden hükümetlere uyarı: Yapay zekânın kabiliyetleri abartılıyor

> **Outlet:** [[Donanım Haber]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** Donanım Haber (staff) · **Date:** 2026-06-08 · **Language:** Turkish · **Country:** [[Turkey (country)\|Turkey]]

**Link:** https://www.donanimhaber.com/matematikcilerden-hukumetlere-uyari-yapay-zekaya-kanmayin--206567

## Summary

Turkish tech news site reports on the 2026 "Leiden Yapay Zekâ ve Matematik Bildirisi" (Leiden AI and Mathematics Declaration), an 11-page document in which mathematicians warn governments and public institutions about AI companies' exaggerated promises. It explains that AI can generate convincing but incorrect mathematical proofs that could contaminate scientific literature with cascading errors, and that the declaration also addresses military uses, mass surveillance, misinformation, environmental costs, and the unconsented use of mathematicians' work to train AI models, calling for a future shaped by human expertise and academic transparency rather than tech marketing.

## Key quote

> Yapay zekânın matematiksel akıl yürütmede devrim yarattığı yönündeki iddialar gerçeği yansıtmıyor; sistemler kulağa son derece ikna edici gelen ama gerçekte hatalı olan matematiksel kanıtlar üretebiliyor.
