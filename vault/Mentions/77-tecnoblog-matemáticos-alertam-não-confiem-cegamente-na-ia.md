# Matemáticos alertam: não confiem cegamente na IA

> **Outlet:** [[Tecnoblog]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Date:** 2026-06 · **Language:** Portuguese

**Link:** https://tecnoblog.net/noticias/matematicos-alertam-nao-confiem-cegamente-na-ia/

## Summary

Tecnoblog reports on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, a manifesto produced after roughly 60 academics met at Leiden University and published 2 June 2026. The article explains the declaration warns that AI tools can cascade errors through the scientific literature if used uncritically, calls on governments to regulate the AI industry, and asks researchers to be transparent about AI use, not list AI systems as co-authors, and choose tools judiciously, while stressing it does not seek to ban AI in mathematics but to promote careful, responsible use.

## Key quote

> A IA tende a apresentar resultados de forma tão convincente que parece não haver erros, mesmo quando há. (per article summary: AI tends to present results so convincingly that it seems there are no errors, even when there are.)

## Significance

Tecnoblog is one of Brazil's largest and most-read consumer technology news outlets, giving the declaration significant reach with a Portuguese-speaking general/tech audience. The piece is staff-written technology journalism rather than authored by a notable mathematician or AI figure.
