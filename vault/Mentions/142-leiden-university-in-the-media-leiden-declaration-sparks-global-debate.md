# Leiden Declaration sparks global debate on the future of mathematics and AI

## Summary

Official Leiden University "in the media" curated roundup page reporting how the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics (published June 2026, origins at a Sept 2025 Lorentz Center workshop, endorsed by the International Mathematical Union) sparked a wider global conversation about reliability, authorship and responsibility in the age of AI. It notes 2,300+ signatories worldwide and aggregates links to coverage in Dutch outlets (Leidsch Dagblad, de Volkskrant, NU.nl, Trouw, NRC) and international media (New York Times, Scientific American, Nature, The Indian Express, India Today, Ars Technica, Phys.org, Irish Times, NetEase). Names Leiden researchers David Holmes and Rodrigo Ochigame and quotes honorary doctor Robbert Dijkgraaf.

## Key quote

> Scientific research is in the midst of a major transformation through the impact of AI, and mathematics is one of the disciplines most fundamentally affected.
