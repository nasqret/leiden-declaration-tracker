# End of Civilization News

> **Outlet:** [[Not Even Wrong (Peter Woit's blog)]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Peter Woit · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

**Link:** https://www.math.columbia.edu/~woit/wordpress/?p=15705

## Summary

Peter Woit's post leads with the same-day release of the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, praising it as "an excellent attempt to identify the new threats to the intellectual culture of the mathematics community." He frames it within his broader argument that AI companies' interest in solving math problems (e.g., Erdos conjectures) is driven by hundreds of billions of dollars in corporate/IPO stakes (OpenAI, Anthropic) rather than genuine advancement of mathematical understanding. He links directly to leidendeclaration.ai and quotes signatory endorsements (Peter Scholze, Kevin Buzzard).

## Key quote

> The big AI/math news is the release today of the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. It's an excellent attempt to identify the new threats to the intellectual culture of the mathematics community and begin a discussion of what to do about them.
