## Summary

An institutional announcement from the UK's Academy for the Mathematical Sciences publicizing the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, which grew out of a 2025 Lorentz Center workshop in Leiden. It frames the Declaration as a call to action with recommendations for individuals, institutions, government, and industry to address risks AI poses to mathematical research (proof accuracy/reliability, attribution, peer review, research-priority distortion), and notes it is supported by the International Mathematical Union.

## Key quote

> The message of the Leiden Declaration is clear: AI offers major opportunities, but without deliberate choices and shared responsibility, it may place the foundations of science under pressure.
