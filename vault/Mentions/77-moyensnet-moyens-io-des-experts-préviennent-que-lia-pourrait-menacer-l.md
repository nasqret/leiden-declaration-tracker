# Des experts préviennent que l'IA pourrait menacer les mathématiques et appellent à des normes

> **Outlet:** [[Moyens.net (Moyens I-O)]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Date:** 2026-06 · **Language:** French

**Link:** https://www.moyens.net/ia/ia-menace-mathematiques-normes-experts-previennent/

## Summary

French-language tech blog article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, an 11-page document produced by a working group of 16 mathematicians over eight months following a September 2025 conference at Leiden University and in consultation with the International Mathematical Union (IMU). It explains that the declaration warns AI threatens the characteristic values of mathematical research—producing plausible but unreliable proofs, failing to properly cite synthesized human work, and pressuring peer review—and calls for norms on transparency, verification, attribution and human responsibility rather than banning AI.
