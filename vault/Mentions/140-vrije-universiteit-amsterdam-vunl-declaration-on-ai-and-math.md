Official news article on the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam website (English-language news section) reporting on the 2026 "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics." The piece explicitly covers THIS declaration: it highlights the involvement of two VU Amsterdam mathematicians, Sander Dahmen and Alain Chavarri Villarello, in the international working group behind the declaration. It describes mathematicians' concerns about AI's impact on authorship, responsibility, and verification of proofs, notes the declaration gained over 2,000 signatures within a week (including seven Fields Medalists), received coverage in The New York Times, Scientific American and Science, and was formally endorsed by the International Mathematical Union.
