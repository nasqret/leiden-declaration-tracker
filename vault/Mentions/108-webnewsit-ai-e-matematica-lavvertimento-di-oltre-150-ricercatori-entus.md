## Summary

Italian tech news article covering the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, reporting that over 150 mathematicians and theoretical computer scientists signed a call for safeguards on AI in mathematical research, stressing that scientific discoveries require independent peer review rather than corporate press announcements. It discusses AI-generated proofs, hallucinations, attribution problems, and concentration of computational power among tech companies, using the OpenAI-Erdős conjecture case as an example.

## Key quote

> l'entusiasmo per i nuovi strumenti non può prendere il posto della verifica indipendente
