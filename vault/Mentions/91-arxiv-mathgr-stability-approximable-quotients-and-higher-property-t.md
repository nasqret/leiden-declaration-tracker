# Stability, approximable quotients, and higher property (T)

> **Outlet:** [[arXiv (math.GR)]] · **Type:** 📄 Preprints · **Author:** Francesco Fournier-Facio · **Date:** 2026-06-08 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

**Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09180

## Summary

A pure mathematics (group theory) preprint constructing finitely presented, Frobenius stable groups with property (T) that are far from property (T_2), and showing property (T_2) does not pass to quotients. Version 2 (8 June 2026) adds a "Post-scriptum: on AI benchmarks" recounting the paper's experience with an AI benchmark that distilled its mathematical statements into flawed multiple-choice questions without human supervision. In the acknowledgements the author explicitly states he subscribes to the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, making this a confirmed scholarly endorsement/mention of the 2026 declaration.
