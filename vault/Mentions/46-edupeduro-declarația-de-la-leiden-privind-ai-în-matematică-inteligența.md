## Summary

Romanian education-news article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, an international declaration endorsed by the International Mathematical Union warning that AI threatens the integrity of mathematical research. It highlights that automated technologies can produce plausible but uncertain or incorrect arguments hard to distinguish from valid proofs, and that scholars call on companies not to use mathematical results without attribution.

## Key quote

> tehnologiile automate „pot produce argumente plauzibile, dar nesigure (sau chiar incorecte) care sunt dificil de distins de dovezi matematice corecte"
