# Perche oltre 150 matematici stanno lanciando un avvertimento sull'AI

> **Outlet:** [[IlSoftware.it]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Michele Nasi · **Date:** 2026-06-08 · **Language:** Italian · **Country:** [[Italy (country)\|Italy]]

**Link:** https://www.ilsoftware.it/matematici-dichiarazione-ridefinisce-ruolo-ai-generativa-ricerca/

## Summary

Italian-language tech news article reporting on the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, signed by 150+ mathematicians and researchers, which warns against overstated claims about AI's mathematical capabilities. It covers concerns about the reliability of AI-generated proofs, lack of transparency around training data, concentration of compute in private hands, and the risk of convincing but hallucinated mathematical arguments entering the scientific literature.

## Key quote

> Una scoperta non diventa vera perche appare in un comunicato stampa, ma perche resiste all'esame rigoroso della comunita.

## Significance

Mid-tier coverage. IlSoftware.it is a long-running, fairly well-trafficked Italian consumer technology news site. The author, Michele Nasi, is the site's founder/editor and a veteran tech journalist, not a mathematician or AI researcher. The article cites IMU vice-president Ulrike Tillmann but does not mention the high-profile signatories (Tao, Scholze) or the working group lead (Portegies). Useful as evidence of Italian-language reach to a general tech audience rather than as authoritative academic commentary.
