# AI is challenging the core values of mathematics: researchers call for urgent action

> **Outlet:** [[Institute of Mathematics and its Applications (IMA)]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United Kingdom (country)\|United Kingdom]]

**Link:** https://ima.org.uk/29314/ai-is-challenging-the-core-values-of-mathematics-researchers-call-for-urgent-action/

## Summary

The Institute of Mathematics and its Applications (IMA), the UK's professional society for mathematicians, announces and extensively summarises the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics — a call by an international group from fifteen universities to protect the core values of mathematics in the age of AI. It notes the declaration does not call for banning AI but urges mathematicians, organisations and governments to implement safeguards on publication quality, attribution and research autonomy, and that it is endorsed by the International Mathematical Union.
