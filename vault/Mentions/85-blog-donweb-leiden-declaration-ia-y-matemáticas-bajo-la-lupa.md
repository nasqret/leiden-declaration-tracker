# Leiden Declaration: IA y matemáticas bajo la lupa

> **Outlet:** [[Blog DonWeb]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Not individually credited (Blog DonWeb editorial) · **Date:** 2026-06-03 · **Language:** Spanish

**Link:** https://blog.donweb.com/leiden-declaration-inteligencia-artificial-matematicas/

## Summary

Spanish-language blog post by web-hosting company DonWeb covering the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics. It explains that the declaration, rather than banning AI, calls for governance frameworks and identifies three core problems: validating proofs humans cannot fully understand, attributing authorship when proprietary models generate results, and distinguishing genuine discoveries from recombinations of existing theorems. It recommends mandatory disclosure of AI use and maintaining rigorous peer review and traceability.

## Key quote

> La matemática no es un negocio de time to market.
