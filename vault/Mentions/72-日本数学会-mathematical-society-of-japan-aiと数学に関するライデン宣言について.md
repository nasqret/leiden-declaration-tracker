# 「AIと数学に関するライデン宣言」について

> **Outlet:** [[日本数学会 (Mathematical Society of Japan)]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Date:** 2026-06-08 · **Language:** Japanese

**Link:** https://www.mathsoc.jp/publicity/news20260608.html

## Summary

An official member notice from the Mathematical Society of Japan announcing the "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics," which it describes as having emerged from the 2025 "Mechanization and Mathematical Research" conference at Leiden University in the Netherlands. The notice characterizes the declaration as setting out the challenges of AI use in mathematical research and guidelines for the future, and directs interested members to the declaration (linking to leidendeclaration.ai).

## Key quote

> この宣言は、数学研究における AI 利用の課題と今後の指針を示したものです。(This declaration sets out the challenges of AI use in mathematical research and guidelines for the future.)

## Significance

High institutional significance: the Mathematical Society of Japan (Nihon Sūgakkai) is Japan's principal national professional body for mathematicians and a member of the IMU. Its formally disseminating the declaration to its membership reflects uptake by a major national mathematical organization, amplifying the declaration within the Japanese mathematics community. No individual author is named (institutional publicity notice).

## Notes

Confirmed as referencing THIS 2026 declaration, not the 2015 Leiden Manifesto for research metrics or any other "Leiden Declaration." The page reproduces the exact English title "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics," cites the September 2025 Leiden University conference, and links to https://leidendeclaration.ai/. The publication date (2026-06-08) is taken from the URL slug (news20260608); no date is printed in the page body. The page itself is a brief notice and does not name the IMU endorsement, Lorentz Center, signatories, or Jim Portegies, though surrounding Japanese coverage (Yahoo News, SBBit, Ledge.ai, ITmedia) confirms these details about the same declaration.
