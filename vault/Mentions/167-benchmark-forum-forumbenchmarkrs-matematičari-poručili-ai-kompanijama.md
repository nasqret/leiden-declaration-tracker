## Summary

A Benchmark Forum thread reposting the Benchmark.rs news article about the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics. It reports that the international mathematics community published the declaration warning that AI threatens principles of mathematical research, from attribution and copyright to the reliability of proofs, and that researchers should disclose AI use, retain responsibility for accuracy, and not assign authorship to AI systems.

## Key quote

> Međunarodna matematička zajednica objavila je Leiden deklaraciju ... dokument koji upozorava da veštačka inteligencija ugrožava principe matematičkog istraživanja, od atribucije i autorskih prava do pouzdanosti dokaza.
