# Mathematics in the age of artificial intelligence: A primer on key topics in the mathematical sciences that underpin AI

> **Outlet:** [[Academy for the Mathematical Sciences]] · **Type:** 🎓 Academic & scholarly · **Author:** David Leslie et al. · **Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United Kingdom (country)\|United Kingdom]]

**Link:** https://www.acadmathsci.org.uk/maths-in-the-age-of-ai-why-the-mathematical-sciences-matter-more-than-ever/

## Summary

A primer/report from the Academy for the Mathematical Sciences (Leslie et al.; DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20538963) on the key mathematical topics that underpin AI, arguing that mathematics provides the foundations of modern AI and that deeper theoretical understanding is essential for reliability, interpretability, optimisation, uncertainty, safety and robustness. It is part of the Academy's AI-and-mathematics work — the same body that endorsed the Leiden Declaration.

## Key quote

> Mathematics provides the foundations of modern AI, and further mathematical innovation will be essential to AI's future development.
