## Summary

Polish-language tech blog article reporting on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. It describes the 11-page declaration drawn up by 16 mathematicians in consultation with bodies such as the International Mathematical Union (IMU), warning that AI-generated proofs may be hard to fit into traditional practices of ideation, presentation and validation, and calling on the mathematical community to protect standards of accuracy, transparency and attribution. It notes the declaration does not ban AI but seeks responsible-use guidelines.

## Key quote
