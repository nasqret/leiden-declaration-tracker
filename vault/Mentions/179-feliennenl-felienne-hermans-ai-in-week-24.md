## Summary

In her weekly personal AI roundup newsletter/blog ("AI in week 24"), computer scientist Felienne Hermans flags the Leiden Declaration as good news ("Goed nieuws"), describing it as a statement by a group of mathematicians against the use of AI in their field. She links directly to leidendeclaration.ai/#declaration. The mention is brief but explicit and clearly references the June 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics (not the 2015 Leiden Manifesto), noting mathematicians' concerns about authorship, accountability, and responsibility for the correctness of results.
