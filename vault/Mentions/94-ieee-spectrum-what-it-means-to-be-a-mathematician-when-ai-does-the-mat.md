# What it Means to Be a Mathematician When AI Does the Math

**Link:** https://spectrum.ieee.org/ai-in-mathematics

## Summary

An IEEE Spectrum feature examining how AI's growing mathematical capabilities are forcing mathematicians to reconsider the discipline's purpose and future, presenting three competing visions (AI as a practical tool, human-AI collaboration, and AI as an autonomous oracle). It references the Leiden Declaration as one of the institutions and 'community groups' developing guidelines for how AI should be used in research and publication (linking to leidendeclaration.ai).
