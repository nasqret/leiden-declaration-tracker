# Mathematicians Warn of AI Threats to Profession As Industry Encroaches

> **Outlet:** [[Slashdot]] · **Type:** 🗣️ Forums & discussion · **Author:** BeauHD (Slashdot editor) · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

**Link:** https://science.slashdot.org/story/26/06/02/192206/mathematicians-warn-of-ai-threats-to-profession-as-industry-encroaches

## Summary

Slashdot post (sourced from Ars Technica) covering the 2026 Leiden Declaration, endorsed by the International Mathematical Union and published June 2, 2026, warning that AI could undermine mathematics by flooding the field with plausible-but-flawed proofs, weakening attribution, distorting incentives, and giving tech companies too much influence over research priorities. It enumerates the declaration's five core concerns (proof reliability, attribution, incentive distortion, misleading press-release communication, and research autonomy).

## Key quote

> "The Leiden Declaration is a well-thought-through response to what is currently happening, as AI continues to disrupt this space." — Kevin Buzzard, Imperial College London
