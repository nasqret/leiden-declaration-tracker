# Dichiarazione di Leida su Intelligenza Artificiale e Matematica

> **Outlet:** [[Unione Matematica Italiana (UMI)]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Author:** Luigi Amedeo · **Date:** 2026-06-05 · **Language:** Italian

**Link:** https://umi.dm.unibo.it/2026/06/05/dichiarazione-di-leida-su-intelligenza-artificiale-e-matematica

## Summary

The Italian Mathematical Union's website reports on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, noting it is officially endorsed by the International Mathematical Union and emerged from the September 2025 NIAS-Lorentz workshop on mechanization and mathematical research (about 60 participants from 10 countries), with a working group convened by Jim Portegies of Eindhoven University of Technology. It summarizes the declaration's warning about five threats AI poses to mathematics (unreliable publications, attribution failures, dependency on proprietary tools, overstated results, and loss of research autonomy) and its call to keep human judgment central to mathematics.

## Key quote

> La dichiarazione e ufficialmente sostenuta dall'Unione Matematica Internazionale (IMU).

## Significance

The UMI (Unione Matematica Italiana) is Italy's national mathematical society and the official institutional voice of the Italian mathematics community, giving this an authoritative national-society reach. The post is editorial coverage rather than authored by a notable mathematician; byline shown as Luigi Amedeo. Significance comes from the outlet's institutional standing relaying the IMU-endorsed declaration to Italian mathematicians.

## Notes

Confirmed via WebFetch this is the 2026 Leiden Declaration (AI and mathematics), explicitly distinct from the 2015 Leiden Manifesto for research metrics.
