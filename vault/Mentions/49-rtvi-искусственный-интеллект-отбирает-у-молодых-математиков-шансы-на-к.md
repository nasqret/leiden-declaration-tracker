# Искусственный интеллект отбирает у молодых математиков шансы на карьеру

## Summary

The article reports on AI systems (e.g. "Gauss" from Math Inc., plus OpenAI and Google DeepMind) rapidly solving and formalizing advanced mathematical proofs, raising fears that young mathematicians and PhD students will be squeezed out of academic careers. It references the June 2026 declaration published by mathematicians worldwide that acknowledges AI's benefits but urges caution about tech companies' secrecy regarding their methods, the conflation of mathematical ability with machine superintelligence/AGI, and AI models being trained on cutting-edge research without authors' consent.
