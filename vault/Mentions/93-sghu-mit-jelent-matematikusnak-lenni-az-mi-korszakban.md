## Summary

Hungarian-language science article discussing what it means to be a mathematician in the AI era, prompted by OpenAI and Google DeepMind solving long-standing mathematical problems. It notes the mathematical community's largely conservative stance on AI adoption and argues mathematics involves theory-building beyond mere problem-solving, with AI serving as a tool requiring human critical thinking rather than a replacement. The article explicitly references and hyperlinks the "Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics" (Leideni Nyilatkozat), noting its proposal that AI usage be disclosed in scientific papers and human authorship affirmed.
