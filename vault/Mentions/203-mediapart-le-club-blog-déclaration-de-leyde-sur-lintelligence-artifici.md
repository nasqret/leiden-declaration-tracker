## Summary

A French-language presentation of the Leiden Declaration ('Déclaration de Leyde sur l'intelligence artificielle et les mathématiques') on Mediapart's participatory blog platform (Le Club), published by a dedicated 'declaration-de-leyde' blog account. It lays out the declaration's concerns — AI models producing plausible but unreliable proofs, announcing breakthroughs via company blogs instead of peer review, and failing to attribute the human work they synthesise — and its recommendations for public computing infrastructure and non-proprietary/open models.
