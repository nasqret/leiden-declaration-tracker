## Summary

In this Cross-Check post on AI and higher education, science journalist John Horgan references the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics: he notes that Terence Tao has endorsed it and that the international group of mathematicians warns 'AI is putting fundamental values of the discipline under threat'. He presents the declaration's recommendations as reasonable safeguards but is skeptical they will be widely adopted.
