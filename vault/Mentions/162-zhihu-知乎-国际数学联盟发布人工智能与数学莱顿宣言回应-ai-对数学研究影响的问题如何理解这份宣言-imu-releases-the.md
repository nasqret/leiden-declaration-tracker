## Summary

A Zhihu Q&A discussion thread asking how to interpret the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, which the International Mathematical Union (IMU) endorsed and which responds to AI's impact on mathematical research. The thread and related Zhihu content explain the declaration's origin (the September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University, ~60 participants from 10 countries), its core values (proof certainty, author responsibility, third-party verifiability, community evaluation standards, autonomy in shaping research questions), notes that it does not call for banning AI but for clear norms, and reports Fields Medalist Terence Tao's support and 550+ (later ~1,800) signatories.
