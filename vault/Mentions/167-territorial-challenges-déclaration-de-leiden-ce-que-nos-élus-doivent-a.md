## Summary

Original French editorial/opinion piece on Territorial Challenges, a digital publication covering the digital transformation of territories and French local government (collectivités territoriales). The article explicitly references the Leiden Declaration on AI and mathematics ("La Déclaration de Leiden du 2 juin"), describing it as a solemn warning issued by leading mathematicians, and argues that the declaration must be read, understood and integrated by elected officials shaping policy in local communities. It frames the math/AI declaration as relevant to public administration's adoption of AI. The piece is original commentary, not an auto-translation or scraper mirror, though it does not name Jim Portegies or the IMU directly.

## Key quote
