## Summary

The article reports on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, an 11-page document signed by 150+ mathematicians worldwide warning policymakers not to overestimate AI's capabilities in mathematics. It frames the declaration as a caution against tech-industry hype and recommends governments consult mathematical experts rather than relying on industry press releases when forming AI policy.
