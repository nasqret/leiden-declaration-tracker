## Summary

A Spanish-language article in El Español's technology vertical Omicrono covering the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, described as a community initiative backed by the International Mathematical Union. It reports the declaration's core concern that current AI/computational techniques can generate arguments that appear correct but are unreliable and even incorrect, while difficult to distinguish from valid proofs, and stresses the values of human rigor, attribution, transparency, and peer-review integrity.
