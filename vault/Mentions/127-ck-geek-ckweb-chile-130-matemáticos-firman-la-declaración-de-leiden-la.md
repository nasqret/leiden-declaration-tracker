# 130 matemáticos firman la Declaración de Leiden: la IA amenaza la autonomía de las matemáticas y el rigor de sus pruebas

> **Outlet:** [[CK Geek (CKWeb, Chile)]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** CK Geek (republished from WWWhat's new / Juan Diego Polo) · **Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Language:** Spanish · **Country:** [[Chile (country)\|Chile]]

**Link:** https://ckgeek.ckweb.cl/2026/06/130-matem-firman-la-declaraci-de-leiden.html

## Summary

Spanish-language tech blog article reporting on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics (published 2 June 2026, endorsed by the International Mathematical Union). It explains that AI is framed not as a mere tool but as an external agent that, without deliberate safeguards, could alter what mathematics is, how results are validated, and who decides which questions matter, raising concerns over proof verification, authorship attribution, and originality.

## Key quote

> "Una prueba matemática que nadie puede seguir no es una prueba en el sentido epistemológico que la disciplina ha construido durante siglos."
