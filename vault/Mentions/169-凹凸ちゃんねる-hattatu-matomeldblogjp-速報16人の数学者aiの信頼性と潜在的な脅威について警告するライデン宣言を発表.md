# 【速報】16人の数学者『AIの信頼性と潜在的な脅威』について警告する『ライデン宣言』を発表

> **Outlet:** [[凹凸ちゃんねる (hattatu-matome.ldblog.jp)]] · **Type:** 🗣️ Forums & discussion · **Author:** ななしさん＠発達中 (anonymous poster / 2ch-style thread aggregator) · **Date:** 2026-06-09 · **Language:** Japanese · **Country:** [[Japan (country)\|Japan]]

**Link:** https://hattatu-matome.ldblog.jp/archives/63261898.html

## Summary

A Japanese 2ch-style thread-aggregator (matome) blog post reporting on the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, released 2 June 2026 by mathematicians and endorsed by the International Mathematical Union. It explains that rather than banning AI, the declaration aims to protect core mathematical values (proof reliability, peer review, human accountability) and lists five main threats: proliferation of plausible-but-wrong proofs, attribution/copyright issues, AI-dependency inequality, results hyped via press releases instead of peer review, and loss of research autonomy.

## Key quote

> 証明の信頼性、著作権や貢献の帰属、研究の自律性などに影響を及ぼす (affecting proof reliability, attribution of contributions and copyright, and research autonomy)
