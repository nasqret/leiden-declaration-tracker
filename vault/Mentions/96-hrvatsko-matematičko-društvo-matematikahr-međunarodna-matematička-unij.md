## Summary

The Croatian Mathematical Society reports that the International Mathematical Union officially endorsed the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, released 2 June 2026, which grew out of a September 2025 conference at Leiden University attended by ~60 experts from 10 countries. The article outlines the declaration's concerns about AI threatening core mathematical values (proof, clarity, attribution, independent verification, community accountability) and its recommendations on transparent tool disclosure and editorial standards.

## Key quote

> The IMU described the declaration as a "timely, serious and balanced contribution" encouraging transparent community action aligned with disciplinary values.
