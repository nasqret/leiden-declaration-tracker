# Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics

> **Outlet:** [[London Mathematical Society]] · **Type:** 🏛️ Institutions & societies · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United Kingdom (country)\|United Kingdom]]

**Link:** https://www.lms.ac.uk/news/leiden-declaration-on-ai-and-mathematics

## Summary

The London Mathematical Society news page announces the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, describing it as a statement developed following a 2025 workshop at the Lorentz Center in Leiden. It summarizes the declaration's concerns about AI's role in mathematics: reliability of automatically generated results, attribution of outputs from proprietary trained models, and impacts on publication practices and peer review, along with recommendations for researchers, professional bodies, funders, and policymakers.

## Key quote

> It highlights concerns about how AI may affect established practices in mathematical research.
