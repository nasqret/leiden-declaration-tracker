Smithsonian Magazine "Smart News" article by Ellen Wexler reporting that an OpenAI model identified a better strategy than the long-assumed optimal solution to an 80-year-old unit-distance problem posed by Paul Erdős in 1946. The piece situates the AI achievement within broader concerns about AI in mathematics and explicitly cites the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, published June 2, 2026, describing it as a set of recommendations/guidelines for researchers, policymakers and organizations on the ethical use of AI in mathematics.
