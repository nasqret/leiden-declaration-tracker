## Summary

Japanese feature article (syndicated to Yahoo!ニュース from SB Creative's Business+IT, page 2 of a multi-page piece) on whether a "singularity" has arrived in mathematics as AI proves long-standing open problems. It reports that mathematicians issued the "ライデン宣言" (Leiden Declaration), endorsed by a vice-president of the International Mathematical Union and signed by 150+ mathematicians worldwide, warning against overhyped AI use and stressing human verification, author responsibility, proper attribution, and concerns about AI generating plausible-but-false proofs.
