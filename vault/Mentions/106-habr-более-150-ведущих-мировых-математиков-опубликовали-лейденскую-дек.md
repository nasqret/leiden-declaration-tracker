# Более 150 ведущих мировых математиков опубликовали «Лейденскую декларацию»

> **Outlet:** [[Habr]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Habr news desk (unattributed) · **Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Language:** Russian

**Link:** https://habr.com/ru/news/1047126/

## Summary

Russian-language Habr news item reporting that more than 150 leading mathematicians published the 2026 Leiden Declaration, arguing that big tech turns fundamental science into a marketing tool by announcing AI "breakthroughs" via press releases instead of peer review. It covers the declaration's concerns about AI generating plausible-but-wrong proofs, copyright/attribution violations from training data, AI not being a valid author with humans bearing full responsibility, and ethical worries (surveillance, military AI); it notes the declaration calls for open data, independent verification, and accountability rather than abandoning AI.

## Key quote

> ИИ не может быть автором, а вся ответственность за корректность формул и доказательств лежит исключительно на человеке.
