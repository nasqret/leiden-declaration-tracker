# Zaczęło się! AI buja kolebką logiki

> **Outlet:** [[MamStartup]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Przemysław Zieliński · **Date:** 2026-06-06 · **Language:** Polish · **Country:** [[Poland (country)\|Poland]]

## Summary

Polish-language article covering the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics (released 2 June 2026, endorsed by the International Mathematical Union and signed by mathematicians including Fields Medalists Terence Tao and Peter Scholze). It lays out the declaration's five identified threats to mathematics (unverifiable "invisible" errors in AI-generated proofs, IP theft and lack of citation, unequal access to compute, marketing hype over rigorous science, and loss of research autonomy), and frames it ahead of the International Congress of Mathematicians in Philadelphia in July 2026.

## Key quote

> The aim of mathematical research is human understanding of mathematics, so it can only develop within a community of human researchers. (Peter Scholze)
