# 突破还是炒作？2000余位学者签署《人工智能与数学莱顿宣言》，警示AI对基础科学的「利用」与「局限」

## Summary

Chinese-language article reporting that over 2,000 scholars have signed the 2026 "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics," framing it around the "breakthrough or hype" debate over AI's role in basic science. It explains the declaration's warning that uncontrolled automation threatens both the methodology and the meaning of mathematics — including AI generating plausible-but-incorrect proofs that humans struggle to verify, compromised peer review, threats to researcher autonomy, and AI developers' commercial incentives to exaggerate capabilities.

## Key quote

> AI has opened exciting new opportunities, but also raises questions requiring scrutiny. Mathematics research's future must be guided by human judgment, fair transparent practices, and shared global values.
