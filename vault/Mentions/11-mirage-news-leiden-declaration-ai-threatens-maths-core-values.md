# Leiden Declaration: AI Threatens Math's Core Values

> **Outlet:** [[Mirage News]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English

**Link:** https://www.miragenews.com/leiden-declaration-ai-threatens-maths-core-1684735/

## Summary

Republishes a Leiden University press release on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, warning that AI poses five threats to mathematics: unreliable results, improper attribution/copyright violations, researcher dependence and inequality, overhyping of results, and loss of disciplinary autonomy. Notes the declaration grew out of a September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop, was convened by a working group led by Jim Portegies (TU/e), and is endorsed by the International Mathematical Union (IMU).

## Key quote

> The goal of mathematical research is human understanding of mathematics, so mathematics can only thrive in a community of human researchers. (attributed to Peter Scholze, Max Planck Institute)
