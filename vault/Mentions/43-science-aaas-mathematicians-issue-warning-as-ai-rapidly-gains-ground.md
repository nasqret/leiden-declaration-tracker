# Mathematicians issue warning as AI rapidly gains ground

> **Outlet:** [[Science (AAAS)]] · **Type:** 🧪 Science & tech magazines · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English

**Link:** https://www.science.org/content/article/mathematicians-issue-warning-ai-rapidly-gains-ground

## Summary

News article in Science (AAAS) reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, in which mathematicians warn that the rapid deployment of AI in research jeopardizes the field's core values and culture. It explains the declaration's concerns that AI-generated papers could overwhelm peer review with low-quality work, make it hard to assign proper credit for discoveries, and disadvantage researchers who choose not to use AI tools; it notes the declaration is endorsed by the International Mathematical Union and open for signatures.

## Key quote

> New declaration argues the technology jeopardizes the field's values and culture.

## Significance

High influence: Science (AAAS) is one of the world's most prominent and authoritative general-science publications, giving the Leiden Declaration major mainstream scientific visibility. The article was widely picked up (Hacker News, Slashdot/Lemmy, and multiple republishers), amplifying its reach.
