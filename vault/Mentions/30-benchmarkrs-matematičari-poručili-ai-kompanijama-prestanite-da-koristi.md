# Matematičari poručili AI kompanijama: Prestanite da koristite naš rad bez dozvole

> **Outlet:** [[Benchmark.rs]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** Ivan Mančić · **Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Language:** Serbian · **Country:** [[Serbia (country)\|Serbia]]

**Link:** https://benchmark.rs/vesti/nauka-i-tehnologija/matematicari-porucili-ai-kompanijama-prestanite-da-koristite-nas-rad-bez-dozvole/

## Summary

Serbian tech news article covering the Leiden Declaration, in which the international mathematical community (endorsed by the International Mathematical Union) warns that current AI development threatens foundational principles of mathematics. It details the core concerns: unreliable AI-generated arguments with subtle errors, training on published research without consent and lack of attribution, and threats to research autonomy, and criticizes AI companies for promoting mathematical results via marketing before peer review.

## Key quote

> trenutni način razvoja i primene AI sistema ugrožava nekoliko temeljnih principa na kojima matematika počiva (the current way AI systems are developed and applied threatens several foundational principles on which mathematics rests)
