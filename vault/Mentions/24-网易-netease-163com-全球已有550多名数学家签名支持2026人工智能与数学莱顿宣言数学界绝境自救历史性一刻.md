# 全球已有550多名数学家签名支持2026《人工智能与数学莱顿宣言》数学界绝境自救历史性一刻

> **Outlet:** [[网易 (NetEase - 163.com)]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** Republished from 新浪财经 (Sina Finance); individual author not identified · **Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Language:** Chinese

**Link:** https://www.163.com/dy/article/KUIBUJMJ05568W0A.html

## Summary

Chinese-language news article (carried on NetEase, originally from Sina Finance) reporting that the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, born from a September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University and endorsed by the International Mathematical Union, had drawn 550+ mathematician signatories including Fields Medalists Terence Tao and Peter Scholze. It frames the declaration as a historic act of self-rescue by the math community, addressing AI's threats to peer review integrity, attribution/copyright, AI training on mathematical literature, and human oversight.

## Key quote

> 数学研究的本质是实现人类对数学的理解 (the essence of mathematical research is advancing human understanding of mathematics)

## Significance

NetEase (163.com) is one of China's largest news/portal platforms, and the piece originated from Sina Finance, another major outlet, giving the declaration significant Chinese-language reach. No notable mathematician or AI figure is the author; it is mainstream news aggregation/reporting. Note the lower 550+ signatory count reflects an early-June snapshot, before the total grew toward 2,500+.

## Notes

Confirmed as referencing THIS 2026 AI-and-mathematics Leiden Declaration (not the 2015 Leiden Manifesto for research metrics): matches Lorentz Center/Leiden University September 2025 origin, IMU endorsement, and signatories Terence Tao and Peter Scholze.
