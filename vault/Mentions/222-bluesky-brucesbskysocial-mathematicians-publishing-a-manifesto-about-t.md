## Summary

Science fiction author and futurist Bruce Sterling shared a Bluesky post on 23 June 2026 linking directly to the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics (leidendeclaration.ai/#declaration). He frames it as "Mathematicians publishing a manifesto about the effects of AI math-slop on the truth of mathematics and on the structure of their discipline and the academy," and draws an analogy comparing the situation to what AI is doing to musicians: "This reads like the clear and euclidean math version of whatever happens to musicians."
