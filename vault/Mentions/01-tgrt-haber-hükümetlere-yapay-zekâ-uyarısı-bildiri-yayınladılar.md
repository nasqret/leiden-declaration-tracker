## Summary

Turkish-language news article reporting that more than 150 mathematicians and academics published the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics (an 11-page document), warning governments and public institutions against the exaggerated promises of AI companies about AI's mathematical capabilities. It notes the document grew out of a fall 2025 gathering of ~60 researchers and policymakers at Leiden University's Lorentz Center, and cites Leiden researcher Rodrigo Ochigame on the ethical problem of mathematicians' work being used to train AI models without consent, as well as risks of AI-hallucinated content entering the scientific literature.
