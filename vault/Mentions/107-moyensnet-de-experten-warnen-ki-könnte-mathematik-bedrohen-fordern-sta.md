## Summary

German-language tech blog article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. It explains that the declaration was created by 16 mathematicians in consultation with bodies including the International Mathematical Union (IMU) and Leiden University, grew out of a workshop at the Lorentz Center, and does not ban AI but calls for community standards on how AI is used in mathematical work (proof, verification, attribution).

## Key quote

> Die "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics" verbietet KI nicht, sondern fragt, wie die Gemeinschaft Standards setzen soll, wenn KI in der mathematischen Arbeit eingesetzt wird.
