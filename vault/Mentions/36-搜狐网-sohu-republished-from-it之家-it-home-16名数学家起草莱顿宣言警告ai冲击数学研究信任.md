# 16名数学家起草《莱顿宣言》，警告AI冲击数学研究信任

> **Outlet:** [[搜狐网 (Sohu, republished from IT之家 - IT Home)]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** IT之家 (IT Home staff; byline not individually attributed) · **Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Language:** Chinese · **Country:** [[China (country)\|China]]

**Link:** https://m.sohu.com/a/1032104087_114760

## Summary

Chinese-language news report stating that 16 mathematicians from 15 universities drafted the 2026 Leiden Declaration (《莱顿宣言》), warning that AI threatens core values of mathematical research such as the certainty of proof, authorship responsibility, third-party verifiability, community evaluation standards, and research autonomy. It explains the declaration grew out of the September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University (~60 participants from 10 countries), is endorsed by the IMU, and does not call for banning AI but for establishing clear community norms; it lists threats including hidden errors in AI proofs, uncited reuse of human work, widening resource gaps, media hype, and commercial influence over mathematical priorities.

## Key quote

> 数学研究的核心价值包括证明带来的高确定性、作者责任、第三方可验证性、共同体评价标准，以及自主塑造研究问题的能力。
