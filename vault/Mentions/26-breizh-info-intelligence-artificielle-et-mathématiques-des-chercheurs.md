## Summary

A French-language news article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, which it describes as signed by ~60 mathematicians from 10 countries and endorsed by the International Mathematical Union, drafted following the September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University. It outlines the declaration's five areas of vulnerability (rigor/verification, attribution of work, research incentives, scientific communication, and disciplinary autonomy) and its recommendations for individual researchers, mathematical organizations, governments, and AI companies.
