## Summary

Mathematician Terence Tao announces on Mastodon that the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics is now live and seeking signatories, explaining it grew out of the September 2025 Leiden University (Lorentz Center) workshop on "Mechanization and Mathematical Research." He frames the declaration as an effort to make explicit the mathematical community's goals and values at a time when powerful AI can be set to optimize (or over-optimize) the goals presented to it, and notes the working group's recommendations for mathematicians, institutions, and external organizations.
