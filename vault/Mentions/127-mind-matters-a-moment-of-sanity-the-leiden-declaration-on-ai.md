## Summary

A short, opinionated news brief welcoming the 2026 Leiden Declaration as "a moment of sanity," emphasizing the declaration's warning that AI developers have a strong commercial incentive to overstate the mathematical capabilities of their products and that governments should not "believe the hype." It quotes/relays reporting (citing Phys.org) and names declaration co-author Michael Harris of Columbia University.

> AI developers face "a strong commercial incentive… to overstate the capabilities of their products," the declaration read.
