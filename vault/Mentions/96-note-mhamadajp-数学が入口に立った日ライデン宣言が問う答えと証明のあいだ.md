# 数学が「入口」に立った日——ライデン宣言が問う、答えと証明のあいだ

> **Outlet:** [[note (mhamadajp)]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** mhamadajp · **Date:** 2026-06-06 · **Language:** Japanese

**Link:** https://note.com/mhamadajp/n/n6fadd3e87a2c

## Summary

A Japanese-language blog post on note.com reflecting on the Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics (announced 2 June 2026). It frames the declaration as marking the moment mathematics "stands at the gateway" of the AI era, where answers can be produced but the community's ability to verify why they are correct is at risk. It lays out the declaration's core concerns—plausible-but-wrong proofs increasing peer-review burden, unclear attribution/authorship, unequal access to AI, publicity preceding peer review, and research agendas being shaped by AI companies—and stresses that the declaration is not about banning AI but about preserving human understanding, responsibility, verification, and fairness.

## Key quote

> 答えは出る。しかし「なぜ正しいのか」を共同体が確認できるか—— (Answers emerge, but can the community verify "why it is correct"—)
