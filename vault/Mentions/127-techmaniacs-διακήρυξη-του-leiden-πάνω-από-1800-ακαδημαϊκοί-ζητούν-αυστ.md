## Summary

Greek-language tech news article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, noting that over 1,800 academics back establishing strict governance rules for AI use in mathematics and scientific research. It describes the declaration's framework (cited as 11 pages with 23 guidelines) addressing researcher transparency, institutional standards, and government regulation to protect peer review, attribution, open access, and human accountability, and notes endorsement by the International Mathematical Union.

## Key quote

> Θα πρέπει να αναφέρουν αν έχει γίνει χρήση κάποιου LLM και τι ακριβώς έχει συνεισφέρει στην έρευνα (Researchers must disclose whether an LLM has been used and exactly what it contributed to the research).
