## Summary

A Greek-language news/analysis article from Protagon.gr, a legitimate Athens-based online news and opinion outlet, examining the impact of AI on mathematics. It explicitly references the "Leiden Declaration" (Διακήρυξη του Λέιντεν) as an international manifesto presented by mathematicians in May/June 2026 with more than 2,300 signatories, examining both the opportunities and risks of AI in mathematics and proposing guidelines for ethical and transparent use. It identifies Rodrigo Ochigame (University of Leiden), a historian of computing, as a contributor to the declaration, citing his view that the mathematical profession may change but will not disappear. The corroborating details (2,300+ signatories, mid-2026 timing, Ochigame as Leiden-based contributor) confirm this is the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, not the 2015 Leiden Manifesto on research metrics.
