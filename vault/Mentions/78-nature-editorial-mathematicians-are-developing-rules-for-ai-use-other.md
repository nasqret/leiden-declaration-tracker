# Mathematicians are developing rules for AI use — other fields should follow

> **Outlet:** [[Nature (Editorial)]] · **Type:** 🎓 Academic & scholarly · **Date:** 2026-06-16 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United Kingdom (country)\|United Kingdom]]

**Link:** https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-026-01881-2

## Summary

An unsigned Editorial in Nature arguing that the mathematics community is right to call for transparency, integrity and fairness to be protected when AI tools are used, and that researchers in other disciplines should learn from its approach. It recounts the September 2025 Leiden University meeting that produced the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, which recognises AI's potential while urging that human judgement, transparency and fairness be safeguarded.
