# Les mathématiciens appellent à la régulation de l'IA et avertissent d'une crise de l'intégrité de la recherche

## Summary

A syndicated news item (originally from Rappler) on the crypto-exchange MEXC's news portal reporting that nearly 2,000 mathematicians from institutions including Harvard, Columbia, Cambridge and Oxford signed the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, which warns that AI threatens core values of mathematics (rigorous proof, attribution, peer review, human understanding). It summarizes the declaration's call for significantly increased public oversight of AI, mandatory disclosure of AI/LLM use in papers, stricter peer review, public computational infrastructure funding, and stronger copyright protections.

## Key quote

> There is a risk that inaccurate AI-generated drafts could clutter the literature with claimed results that are simply wrong.
