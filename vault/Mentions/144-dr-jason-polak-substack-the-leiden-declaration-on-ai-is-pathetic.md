# The Leiden Declaration on AI is pathetic

> **Outlet:** [[Dr. Jason Polak (Substack)]] · **Type:** 📨 Newsletters · **Author:** Jason Polak · **Date:** 2026-06-03 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

**Link:** https://jasonpolak.substack.com/p/the-leiden-declaration-on-ai-is-pathetic

## Summary

Mathematician Jason Polak harshly criticizes the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics as weak and merely performative, saying he will not sign it. He argues the declaration's core recommendation (transparent disclosure of AI/LLM/proof-assistant use) fails to confront the deeper harm AI will do to mathematical learning and independence, and that in a competitive field people will use AI to get ahead regardless of guidelines. He calls for stopping AI use in mathematics rather than adopting cautious disclosure norms.

## Key quote

> It's negatively disruptive regardless of good intentions. The fact is, in a highly competitive field like mathematics, people will use it to get ahead.
