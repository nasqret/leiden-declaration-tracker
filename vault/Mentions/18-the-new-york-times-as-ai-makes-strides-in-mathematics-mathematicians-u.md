# As A.I. Makes Strides in Mathematics, Mathematicians Urge Caution

> **Outlet:** [[The New York Times]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** Siobhan Roberts · **Date:** 2026-06-02 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

**Link:** https://www.nytimes.com/2026/06/02/science/ai-mathematics-leiden-declaration.html

## Summary

Reports on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, in which mathematicians warn that unchecked AI automation threatens the core values of the discipline (proof, verification, attribution, peer review, human oversight). It describes the declaration's guidelines for researchers, policymakers and organizations, including disclosing which AI tools were used, taking responsibility for accuracy, and properly citing prior scholarship that AI may have drawn on, and quotes co-authors on the tension between commercial AI logic and the values of mathematics.

## Key quote

> The tech industry proceeds in accordance with commercial logic, which is antithetical to the values of mathematics.

## Significance

Major, globally influential newspaper of record. Authored by Siobhan Roberts, the NYT's veteran mathematics/science journalist (biographer of John Conway and Donald Coxeter), giving the coverage high visibility and credibility. The piece quotes declaration co-authors Michael Harris (Columbia) and Rodrigo Ochigame (Leiden).
