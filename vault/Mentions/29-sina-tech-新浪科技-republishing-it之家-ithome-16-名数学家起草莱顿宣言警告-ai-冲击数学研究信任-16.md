# 16 名数学家起草《莱顿宣言》，警告 AI 冲击数学研究信任 (16 mathematicians draft the "Leiden Declaration", warning AI undermines trust in mathematical research)

> **Outlet:** [[Sina Tech (新浪科技), republishing IT之家 (ITHome)]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Author:** IT之家 (ITHome); republished by Sina Tech · **Date:** 2026-06-04 · **Language:** Chinese · **Country:** [[China (country)\|China]]

**Link:** https://finance.sina.com.cn/tech/digi/2026-06-04/doc-iniafmmu0520065.shtml

## Summary

A Chinese-language tech news report on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, drafted by 16 mathematicians from 15 universities and grown out of the September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University, backed by the International Mathematical Union (IMU). It explains that the declaration warns AI challenges mathematics' core values, proof reliability, author attribution, third-party verifiability, community evaluation standards, and research autonomy, while also flagging resource disparities favoring researchers with access to advanced proprietary AI. It notes the declaration does not call for banning AI but for clear community norms.

## Key quote

> 数学不只是结果集合，更是培养理解、清晰表达和判断力的人类活动 ("Mathematics is not merely a collection of results, but a human activity that cultivates understanding, clear expression, and judgment.")
