# Leiden Declaration: KI soll die Mathematik nicht untergraben – Forderungen an Forschung und Industrie

## Summary

German-language tech news article on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, reporting that the declaration warns AI-generated proofs lack the transparency and traceability required by mathematical peer review, and that premature dissemination via blogs and press releases can embed false results in the field. It covers the declaration's calls for transparency about AI tool use, stricter peer-review procedures, and public computing infrastructure to reduce dependence on commercial vendors, citing IMU Vice President Ulrike Tillmann on mathematics remaining a deeply human discipline.

## Key quote

> Mathematik müsse eine zutiefst menschliche Aufgabe bleiben – auch wenn KI unterstützend wirkt.
