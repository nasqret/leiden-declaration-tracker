# Matemáticos alertam: não confiem cegamente na IA

> **Outlet:** [[Tecnoblog Comunidade]] · **Type:** 🗣️ Forums & discussion · **Date:** 2026-06 · **Language:** Portuguese · **Country:** [[Brazil (country)\|Brazil]]

## Summary

Portuguese-language Tecnoblog Comunidade forum thread (the community/discussion mirror of the Tecnoblog news article of the same title) reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. It explains that mathematicians who gathered at Leiden University in the Netherlands to discuss the "mechanization" of mathematics warn against blindly trusting AI, noting that AI tools can propagate cascading errors through scientific literature, do not credit the ideas they use, and that companies tout successes without transparency about failures; the declaration calls on researchers to disclose AI use, not list AI as co-author, and asks governments to regulate the AI industry.

## Key quote

> Se simplesmente perguntamos a uma máquina e aceitamos sua resposta cegamente, sem entender o caminho, perdemos nossa independência intelectual.
