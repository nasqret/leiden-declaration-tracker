# AI bedreigt wiskunde, waarschuwen onderzoekers

> **Outlet:** [[Engineers Online]] · **Type:** 🧪 Science & tech magazines · **Author:** Redactie Engineersonline (editorial staff) · **Date:** 2026-06-03 · **Language:** Dutch

**Link:** https://www.engineersonline.nl/ai-bedreigt-wiskunde-waarschuwen-onderzoekers/

## Summary

Dutch trade-media article announcing the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, created at a Leiden University workshop and led by TU/e mathematician Jim Portegies. It describes the declaration's concerns about AI threatening mathematics (unreliable results, missing attribution, dependence on closed commercial AI systems, inflated claims, loss of scientific independence) and notes it calls for responsible-use guidelines and stronger regulation rather than a ban.

## Key quote

> Wat gebeurt er als een wiskundig bewijs niet langer het werk is van een mens, maar van een gesloten AI-model

## Significance

Engineers Online (Engineersonline.nl) is a Dutch engineering/technical trade publication with a professional audience; moderate niche reach within the Netherlands engineering sector. The article is by editorial staff, not a notable mathematician or AI figure, but it cites notable signatories Terence Tao and Robbert Dijkgraaf and the working-group lead Jim Portegies.
