## Summary

Dutch newspaper NRC reports on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics, in which an international group of mathematicians (led by Jim Portegies of TU/e, growing out of the September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop in Leiden) warns that AI is putting the core values of mathematics under pressure. The headline frames a central concern of the declaration: that the involvement of big tech companies risks letting commercial AI methods, rather than deeper mathematical significance, determine which research questions matter.
