# 150 عالما في الرياضيات يحذرون من "الهالة الكبيرة" حول الذكاء الاصطناعي

> **Outlet:** [[Al Jazeera (الجزيرة نت)]] · **Type:** 📰 Newspapers & wire services · **Date:** 2026-06-03 · **Language:** Arabic · **Country:** [[Qatar (country)\|Qatar]]

**Link:** https://www.aljazeera.net/science/2026/6/3/150-عالما-في-الرياضيات-يحذرون-من-الهالة

## Summary

Arabic-language Al Jazeera science article reporting on the 2026 Leiden Declaration on AI and Mathematics, an international scientific statement warning that AI poses ethical and epistemological challenges to mathematics' core values rather than being merely a technical opportunity. It lays out five dangers identified by the declaration: AI-generated proofs that look convincing but contain subtle errors, loss of attribution to original authors, inequality of access to compute/AI, media hype and oversimplification, and erosion of research autonomy due to commercial interests; it stresses the declaration does not reject AI but demands transparent, responsible use with humans retaining final scientific responsibility.

> بيان علمي أصدرته مجموعة دولية من الباحثين للتنبيه إلى أن الذكاء الاصطناعي لا يضع الرياضيات أمام فرصة تقنية فقط (An international scientific statement issued by a group of researchers warning that AI does not place mathematics before a merely technical opportunity)
