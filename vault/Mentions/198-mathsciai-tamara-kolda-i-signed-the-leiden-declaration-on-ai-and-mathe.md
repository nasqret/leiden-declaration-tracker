## Summary

Blog post on MathSci.ai, the personal/company blog of applied mathematician Tamara (Tammy) G. Kolda (independent consultant, ex-Sandia National Labs, member of the U.S. National Academy of Engineering, SIAM and ACM Fellow), announcing that she has signed the 2026 Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics. She endorses the declaration's principles for responsible AI use in mathematical research and shares the statement she submitted with her signature, warning that misuse of AI could pollute the mathematical literature. The post explicitly references THIS 2026 declaration (which originated at a 2025 Lorentz Center workshop at Leiden University and is endorsed by the IMU), not the 2015 Leiden Manifesto for research metrics.

## Key quote

> While AI offers tremendous potential, its misuse will pollute the literature and compromise the foundations on which future mathematical advancements depend.
