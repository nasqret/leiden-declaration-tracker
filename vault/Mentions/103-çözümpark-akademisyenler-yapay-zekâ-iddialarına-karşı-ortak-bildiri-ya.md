# Akademisyenler Yapay Zekâ İddialarına Karşı Ortak Bildiri Yayınladı

> **Outlet:** [[ÇözümPark]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Gürkan Küçükali · **Date:** 2026-06-09 · **Language:** Turkish

**Link:** https://www.cozumpark.com/yapay-zeka-matematik-tartismasi-buyudu/

## Summary

Turkish IT-community article reporting on the "Leiden Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics Declaration" (an 11-page statement signed by 150+ mathematicians and academics), which warns that AI companies' claims about mathematical achievements are exaggerated and that AI can produce plausible but incorrect proofs whose errors propagate. It urges governments and institutions to base research decisions on scientific validation processes rather than commercial narratives, and raises concerns about transparency in how publications are used to train AI models.

## Key quote

> matematik araştırmalarında alınacak kararların ticari söylemler yerine bilimsel doğrulama süreçlerine dayanmasını istedi ("[the signatories] called for decisions in mathematical research to be based on scientific validation processes rather than commercial narratives")

## Significance

ÇözümPark is a well-known Turkish IT/technology community portal (Microsoft/sysadmin/developer audience) rather than a mainstream news outlet; it has solid reach within the Turkish tech community but is not a major general-press source. The author, Gürkan Küçükali, is a regular ÇözümPark contributor/tech writer, not a notable mathematician or AI researcher. Value here is primarily as evidence of Turkish-language coverage of the declaration.
