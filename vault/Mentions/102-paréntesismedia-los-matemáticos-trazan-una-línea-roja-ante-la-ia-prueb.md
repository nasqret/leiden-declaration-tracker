## Summary

Spanish-language article reporting on the Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics (published 2 June 2026, backed by the International Mathematical Union and shaped at the September 2025 Lorentz Center workshop). It describes how the declaration sets boundaries on AI in mathematics without rejecting the technology, covering proof and verifiability, attribution, peer-review burden, exclusive human responsibility for correctness, and warnings about commercial/tech-company influence on research priorities.

## Key quote

> La responsabilidad por la corrección y adecuación de los argumentos... sigue perteneciendo exclusivamente a los autores humanos.
