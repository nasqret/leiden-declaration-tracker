# The Leiden Declaration: Mathematics, AI, and Making Our Values Explicit

> **Outlet:** [[BLOG@CACM (Communications of the ACM)]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Author:** Mateja Jamnik · **Date:** 2026-06-25 · **Language:** English · **Country:** [[United States (country)\|United States]]

## Summary

A BLOG@CACM post by Mateja Jamnik (Professor of AI, University of Cambridge) — a co-author of the Leiden Declaration — presenting it as a call for the mathematical community to make its values explicit as AI becomes part of mathematical research. It recounts the rapid uptake (1,000+ signatures and the IMU's endorsement within about 24 hours) and summarises recommendations such as disclosing AI use in papers, ensuring AI-assisted work is peer-reviewed, and levelling the playing field between academia and industry.
