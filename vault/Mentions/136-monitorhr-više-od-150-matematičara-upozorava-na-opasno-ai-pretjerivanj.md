# Više od 150 matematičara upozorava na opasno AI pretjerivanje

> **Outlet:** [[Monitor.hr]] · **Type:** ✍️ Blogs · **Date:** 2026-06-09 · **Language:** Croatian · **Country:** [[Croatia (country)\|Croatia]]

**Link:** https://www.monitor.hr/vise-od-150-matematicara-upozorava-na-opasno-ai-pretjerivanje/

## Summary

Croatian tech news article reporting that 150+ prominent mathematicians signed the "Leiden Declaration," warning governments and policymakers not to believe corporate AI marketing hype. It highlights concerns about AI hallucinations contaminating scientific literature, the need for human oversight, and ethical risks of commercializing such models (mass surveillance, warfare, undermining democracy), framed against tech-company claims (e.g., OpenAI) that AI autonomously solved decades-old Erdős problems.

## Key quote

> Više od 150 uglednih matematičara potpisalo je 'Leidensku deklaraciju'... upozorava na etičke rizike komercijalizacije takvih modela.
