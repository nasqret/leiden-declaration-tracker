# Više od 150 matematičara upozorava na opasnosti preuveličavanja sposobnosti AI-ja

> **Outlet:** [[Bug.hr]] · **Type:** 🧪 Science & tech magazines · **Author:** Sandro Vrbanus · **Date:** 2026-06-08 · **Language:** Croatian · **Country:** [[Croatia (country)\|Croatia]]

**Link:** https://www.bug.hr/umjetna-inteligencija/vise-od-150-matematicara-upozorava-na-opasnosti-preuvelicavanja-sposobnosti-60421

## Summary

Croatian tech outlet Bug.hr reports that mathematicians worldwide signed the "Leiden Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics," warning governments not to believe marketing hype about AI's problem-solving abilities. It notes AI can generate convincing but false mathematical arguments that could contaminate scientific literature, and cites signatories including IMU Vice President Ulrike Tillmann and Leslie Ann Goldberg (Oxford).

## Key quote

> AI models can produce "convincing, but entirely inaccurate arguments which are difficult to distinguish from correct proofs."
